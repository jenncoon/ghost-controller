What review DOES

What it STRUGGLES to do
Create pressure. Force peer negotiation. Ask students to reason before the stakes are real.
Field Observation, Acct 2301, Spring 2025 Field Report

Students arrived at exam week having completed every publisher review assignment. SmartBook. Videos. Everything. On paper, they were prepared.

Then the exam happened, and they blanked on the accounting cycle. They got the mechanics but lost the logic the moment the question was worded differently.

The review existed. The thinking did not.

Station 03, Design Protocol

Your Design Brief

Framework Document, The 3-Decision Rule Apply Now

Every escape room that works pedagogically was built by answering three questions before a single question was written. Most attempts fail, or become trivia games with a timer, because people start with the questions instead of the design.

1
What concept are you testing?
Pick one learning objective from one exam or assessment, not the whole unit. Focus is what makes it work.
2
What does success look like in the room?
Name the win condition before you write a single question. "They crack the case." "They close the vault." Make it tangible.
3
How does it work when the internet is down?
Design in-person first, using paper and printed stations. The online version follows naturally from a solid in-person design.

Your Brief, Field Exercise

Apply the 3 decisions to a course you actually teach. These answers become your reflection card.
